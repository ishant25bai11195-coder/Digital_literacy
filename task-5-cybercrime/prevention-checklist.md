# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

---

## 🔐 General Safety Tips
- Use strong and unique passwords  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)  
- Avoid clicking unknown or suspicious links  
- Download apps only from trusted sources  
- Keep your devices and apps updated  

---

## 💳 Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- Never share your UPI PIN or OTP  
- Do not scan unknown QR codes  
- Always verify the receiver before payment  

---

## 📢 Reporting Cybercrime
- 🌐 National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- 📞 Helpline Number: 1930  

---

# 📝 Reflection

Poor awareness of digital payment systems can easily lead to financial loss. While researching this topic, I was surprised to learn that many users think entering a UPI PIN is required to receive money, whereas it actually authorizes a payment. This simple misunderstanding is widely used by scammers.

As a result, I have become more careful while using online payment apps. One habit I will change is that I will always verify any payment request before approving it and avoid interacting with unknown links or messages. Being cautious and informed can help prevent such cybercrimes.
