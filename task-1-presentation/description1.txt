For Task 1, I created a digital literacy awareness infographic using Canva. The aim of the design was to present important concepts of digital literacy in a simple and visually appealing manner. The infographic includes key topics such as the meaning of digital literacy, useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, email etiquette, and building a professional online presence.

While designing the infographic, I focused on keeping the content clear and easy to understand. I used a structured layout with different sections to organize the information properly. Each section highlights essential points like using strong passwords, avoiding suspicious links, and maintaining a professional profile on platforms like LinkedIn.

One interesting aspect of this task was learning how to present information visually instead of writing long paragraphs. The most challenging part was selecting the right content and keeping it short while still covering all important points. Overall, this task helped me understand the importance of digital literacy and how it can be effectively communicated through design.
